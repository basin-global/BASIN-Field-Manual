# STREAMS

STREAMS are the unit of account (stocks & flows of natural capital in $'s) and value in the BASIN Protocol. STREAMS are formally called **Certificates of Ensurance**.

As ERC-1155 contracts, STREAMS are semi-fungible and represent the unique and expansive value of each token type, but are also 1:1 certificates of the BASIN Protocol.
